Are you really insured?

Did you know that 1 in 5 drivers globally who file car accident claims are left stranded because insurers say there’s “not enough evidence”? From missing photos to absent police reports, innocent drivers are footing the bill for crashes they didn’t cause.

Gobal Reality Check:
  • 10% of U.S. claims are denied due to lack of evidence (III, 2020)
  • 7% of UK motorists face rejected claims over "insufficient proof" (ABI, 2021)
  • 20% of SA drivers report rejected claims due to missing evidence (Old Mutual, 2022)
